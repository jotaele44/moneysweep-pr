# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
Contract-Sweeper community a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of background or identity. We pledge to act and interact in
ways that contribute to an open, diverse, and healthy community.

## Our standard

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1** as its code of conduct.
The full text — including examples of acceptable behavior, unacceptable behavior, and
the enforcement guidelines maintainers follow — is published at:

- https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

By participating, contributors are expected to uphold that standard.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (the repository, issues, pull
requests, and discussions) and when an individual is officially representing the
project in public spaces.

## Reporting and enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported privately to the project
maintainer at **jorge.gonzalez44@upr.edu**. All reports will be reviewed and
investigated promptly and fairly, and the maintainer will respect the privacy and
security of the reporter.

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this standard and may take
appropriate corrective action — following the enforcement guidelines in the linked
Contributor Covenant — in response to any behavior they deem inappropriate.
